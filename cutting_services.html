Cutting Services

The centerpiece of our sewing operation is an industry-leading fabric cutting machine, the Pathfinder K5000. We invested in this state-of-the-art technology to ensure the highest quality cut parts and accuracy for our customers.

In addition, we offer fabric cutting services to other sewing contractors that are seeking to increase their own productivity.

State-of-the-art equipment

Our fabric cutting services are accurate, reliable and designed to meet your requirements. Features of the Pathfinder K5000 include:

  • patterns imported effortlessly from our advanced digitizing pattern system
  • maximum fabric utilization
  • variable speed control and automatic knife sharpening, ensuring optimum efficiency even with the most difficult of fabrics
  • single ply, low ply or multi-ply cutting
  • accommodation of patterns up to 200 feet long
  • graphic display and comprehensive management console
  • self diagnostics and cutting history log

Cutting services for sewing contractors

Since we introduced the Pathfinder to our operations, we have increased our in-house productivity, reduced fabric waste and achieved faster turnaround times. Through the use of our contract cutting services, you can integrate similar productivity gains into your pricing and delivery projections.
